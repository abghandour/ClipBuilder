Welcome to Clip Builder

This installer will:

  • Install Clip Builder into your Applications folder
  • Open a Terminal window after installation that installs everything else the app needs:
    • Homebrew (if not already installed)
    • ffmpeg — the video engine
    • Node.js and the three AI provider CLIs (Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Codex CLI)
  • Guide you through signing in to each AI provider (now or later — your choice)
  • Help you create your first brand profile

You may be asked for your Mac password during dependency installation. An internet connection is required.
